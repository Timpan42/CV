About Me

Hello, I'm Tim Fagerdal, a game programmer from northern Sweden, living in Vännäs. Currently a game programming student at FutureGames, Umeå.

I love playing games, and I want others to get the same feeling from the games I've been a part of, and I find it super satisfying to solve problems.

Outside the world of video games, I like to listen to music, mainly deathcore and metalcore. Lorna Shore's "Pain Remains" album is amazing, and Kardashev is also one of my favorite bands.

I like to exercise, mostly jogging and weight training, finding it rewarding but tuff, and I also like to sing sometimes, even if I'm not that good at it.
